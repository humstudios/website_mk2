About Hum

Hum Studios is a team of writers who’ve worked at Disney and Universal Studios who joined forces with a group of BAFTA award-winning animators. Together, we wanted to tell cartoon stories that merged the fun and humor of traditional cartoons with the interactive potential of modern technology.

With our kids loving pet stories and animal stories, we created an engaging interactive digital book that combines the educational value of the best preschool apps with the sheer fun of stories about pets.
