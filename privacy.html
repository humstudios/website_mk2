Privacy Notice

What we collect and why

  • Messages you send us (e.g., name, email, message content) when you use our contact form — so we can reply and keep basic records. We also use Cloudflare Turnstile on the form to prevent spam and abuse.
  • Consent and preference data: your cookie decision (hum_consent_prefs) and, if you allow it, your theme preference (hum_theme).
  • Basic site metrics via Cloudflare Web Analytics: aggregated, cookie‑free counters (no cross‑site tracking).
  • Technical logs (short‑lived): standard server and security logs to keep the site reliable and secure.

Our legal bases

  • Legitimate interests — running and securing our website (including Turnstile), replying to enquiries, and measuring basic site performance with cookie‑free analytics.
  • Contract — when you request information or services from us and we need your details to respond.
  • Consent — only for the optional theme preference cookie; you can change your choice at any time.

Who processes your data for us

  • Cloudflare, Inc. — website delivery, security (including Turnstile), and privacy‑focused analytics.
  • Resend, Inc. — email delivery for contact form messages.

We choose providers with strong security standards. Some processing may occur outside the UK/EEA; when it does, we use appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses).

How long we keep data

  • Contact form messages: up to six months.
  • Consent preference cookie: up to one year.
  • Theme cookie: up to one year.
  • Security/technical logs: only for a short period needed to keep the site secure.

Your rights

You can ask us for a copy of your data, to correct or delete it, to limit or object to how we use it, and to transfer it. Where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time. We’ll respond without undue delay (and within one month).

Guidance on your rights is available from the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

How to complain

Please contact us first and we’ll try to resolve it. You can also complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).
