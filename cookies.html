Cookies Policy

This site uses only essential and user-chosen cookies — nothing for tracking or advertising.

Essential cookies

These are required to run the site or remember a choice you’ve made.

Name Purpose Who sets it Expires
hum_consent_prefs Remembers whether you accepted or declined optional cookies (so we don’t ask every visit). Hum Studios 1 year
Cloudflare Turnstile security tokens Helps verify genuine form submissions and protect the site from abuse; used only for security. Cloudflare (Turnstile) Short‑lived

Turnstile is the small verification on our contact form. It may set a token on Cloudflare’s domains purely to provide this security feature. We treat this as strictly necessary.

Optional cookies

Your choice.

Name Purpose Who sets it Expires
hum_theme Remembers your selected theme (Light/Dark/Auto) across visits. If you decline, we’ll still show your chosen theme for the current session only. Hum Studios 1 year

Declining optional cookies does not affect your ability to use the site. You’ll still be able to read, browse, and contact us normally.

Analytics (no cookies)

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to count visits and basic page performance. It does not set cookies or use local storage and doesn’t track you across sites. We use the aggregated reports to improve the site.

Managing your choices

You can change cookie settings at any time. You can also control or delete cookies via your browser settings. Blocking strictly necessary cookies may affect site features (like forms).

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we’ll update the date below and summarise the change here.
