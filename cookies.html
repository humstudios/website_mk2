This Cookie Policy explains how Hum Studios ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (“the Website”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how and why we use cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to ensure websites work efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owner.

How We Use Cookies

We use only the minimum number of cookies necessary to ensure our website functions properly. We also make limited use of Google Analytics to count page views, helping us understand how visitors interact with our site so we can improve usability and performance.

We do not use cookies for advertising, profiling, or personal tracking purposes.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary: Required for basic website functionality and security. Duration: Session or short‑term.
  • Analytics (Google Analytics): Used to anonymously measure page views and usage patterns. Duration: Persistent.

About Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics with IP anonymisation enabled. This means no personally identifiable information is stored or shared with Google. The data we collect through Google Analytics is used solely for the purpose of improving our website’s performance and user experience.

Learn more: Google Analytics – Safeguarding your data.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

On your first visit to our site, you may be asked to accept or decline analytics (optional) cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by adjusting your browser settings or clearing your cookies.

Most web browsers allow some control of cookies through browser settings. Please note that blocking strictly necessary cookies may affect your ability to use parts of our site.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our services. The “Effective Date” at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
