Our first adventure: No Pets Allowed!

Jessie is a young girl who loves animals but lives in a building where no pets are allowed. When the local zoo closes down and an elephant and its friends turn up at Jessie’s apartment looking for a new place to live, Jessie is over the moon – but she needs to keep the animals hidden from the neighbors.

How our digital books work

“No Pets Allowed!” is the first in Hum Studios’ interactive book series. Our books work just like traditional ones, with the pages turning when you swipe the screen on your tablet or phone. Unlike a regular book, though, if you tap the screen, the characters come alive and play out the scene! You can have the app narrate the story or read the book aloud with your child, with a playful music soundtrack if you want it.

It’s a game as well as a book

Find the butterflies! Hidden within the story are five butterflies. As your child reads the adventure, they’re invited to find all of them! The great thing about the book being digital is every time your child reads it, the butterflies are hidden in different places.

Frequently asked questions

Is it safe for young kids?

Yes. There are no ads or pop-ups, no third-party trackers, no chat features, and nothing more to buy once you’ve unlocked the story – nothing interrupts the story.

How much does it cost, and is it a subscription?

Just $2.99, as a one-off payment. There’s no subscription and no recurring charges – buy once, own it forever.

What age is it for?

It’s designed for preschoolers and young readers who love animal stories, and can be enjoyed solo or read together with a parent.

Can I try it before buying?

Yes! Download the first 9 pages for free to meet Jessie and all her animal friends.

Is the app noisy?

No. There are just two gentle sound effects – a soft buzz when you tap a butterfly, and a happy “ta-da!” when all five are found – plus optional narration and a music soundtrack you can switch on or off. No blaring noise, no jarring surprises.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once the story’s downloaded you don’t need an internet connection, so it’s perfect for car rides, flights and holidays.

Can the app read the story aloud?

Yes! You can have the app narrate the story, or read it aloud together with your child – with a playful music soundtrack if you want it.

Was No Pets Allowed! made using AI?

No – it’s written, illustrated and animated by a team who’ve worked at Disney and Universal, alongside BAFTA award-winning animators.

Is it available on Android?

Not yet – it’s currently available on the App Store for iPhone and iPad.
