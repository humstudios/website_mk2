Our first adventure: No Pets Allowed!

Jessie is a young girl who loves animals but lives in a building where no pets are allowed. When the local zoo closes down and an elephant and its friends turn up at Jessie’s apartment looking for a new place to live, Jessie is over the moon—but she needs to keep the animals hidden from the neighbors.

It's an endearing and comical adventure that’s perfect if your child likes elephant stories, crocodile stories, or even penguin stories. Download the first 10 pages for free and meet Jessie and her animal friends.

How our digital books work

“No Pets Allowed!” is the first in Hum Studios’ interactive book series. Our books work just like traditional ones, with the pages turning when you swipe the screen on your tablet or phone. Unlike a regular book, though, if you tap the screen, the characters come alive and play out the scene! You can have the app narrate the story or read the book aloud with your child, with a playful music soundtrack if you want it.

It's a game as well as a book

Find the butterflies! Hidden within the story are five butterflies. As your child reads the adventure, they’re invited to find all of them! The great thing about the book being digital is every time your child reads it, the butterflies are hidden in different places.

No ads, no subscriptions or hidden charges, ever!

As parents ourselves, we wanted to create a fun interactive book for kids that was also worry-free for their parents. That’s why our books are ad and subscription-free. Once you download our book, it’s yours forever, simple as that. You don’t even need to have an internet connection to use it.

It’s fun, it’s educational, it’s safe – and it’s only $2.99!
