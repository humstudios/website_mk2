Our first adventure: No Pets Allowed!

Jessie is a young girl who loves animals but lives in a building where no pets are allowed. When the local zoo closes down and an elephant and its friends turn up at Jessie’s apartment looking for a new place to live, Jessie is over the moon – but she needs to keep the animals hidden from the neighbours.

It’s an endearing and comical adventure that’s perfect if your child likes elephant stories, crocodile stories, or even penguin stories. Download the first 9 pages for free and meet Jessie and her animal friends.

How our digital books work

“No Pets Allowed!” is the first in Hum Studios’ interactive book series. Our books work just like traditional ones, with the pages turning when you swipe the screen on your tablet or phone. Unlike a regular book, though, if you tap the screen, the characters come alive and play out the scene! You can have the app narrate the story or read the book aloud with your child, with a playful music soundtrack if you want it.

It’s a game as well as a book

Find the butterflies! Hidden within the story are five butterflies. As your child reads the adventure, they’re invited to find all of them! The great thing about the book being digital is every time your child reads it, the butterflies are hidden in different places.

Loved by parents

Frequently asked questions

Is my child’s privacy protected?

Yes – and you don’t have to take our word for it. There are no third-party trackers, no analytics and no advertising of any kind, and we collect no personal data at all: Apple’s App Store privacy label lists us as “Data Not Collected”, which you can check yourself on our App Store page.

Is it safe for my child to use on their own?

Yes. No ads or pop-ups, no chat, no links out of the story, and nothing that asks your child to spend money. Hand over the tablet and let them explore – the only surprises are the good kind: characters springing to life, and butterflies to find.

How much does it cost – and can I try it first?

It’s $2.99, once, for the full 27-page adventure – less than a paperback picture book, and it never wears out or gets left on a train. No subscription, no microtransactions, nothing extra to unlock later – and the purchase goes through Apple’s own checkout, so your child can’t buy it by accident. And yes, try it first: the first 9 pages are free, so your child can meet Jessie and her animal friends before you pay anything. If it’s not for them, you’ve lost nothing.

Is this just more screen time?

We’d say it’s the good kind. No Pets Allowed! is a storybook at heart, with a beginning, a middle and a happy ending – just like the books on your child’s shelf. Your child can read it themselves, listen to the narration, or share it aloud with you – a bedtime story that happens to live on a tablet.

What age is it for?

It’s designed for preschoolers and young readers who love animal stories – but like all good picture books, it’s really for kids of all ages. And because the hidden butterflies move to new places every read, it stays fresh long after the first bedtime.

Can the app read the story aloud?

Yes. Your child can listen to the narration, or you can switch it off and read the book aloud together – with an optional music soundtrack either way. It’s all gentle on the ears: no blaring noise, no jarring surprises.

Does it work offline?

Yes, completely. Once it’s downloaded you don’t need an internet connection – perfect for car journeys, flights and holidays, with no data going anywhere.

Was No Pets Allowed! made using AI?

No – none of it. The story, illustrations, animation, music and narration are all made by people – a team who’ve worked at Disney and Universal, alongside BAFTA award-winning animators. It’s a labour of love, and we hope it shows.

Is there a real person I can reach if I need help?

Yes. We’re a small team of parents, not a faceless corporation. Drop us a line through our contact page – whether it’s a question, a problem, or just to tell us what your child thought of the story – and a real human will reply.
